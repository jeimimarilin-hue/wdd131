Cabimas, Zulia, Venezuela

Venezuela is a beautiful country located in the northern part of South America. It is famous for its stunning landscapes, including Angel Falls, the world's highest uninterrupted waterfall, its rich cultural heritage, and warm, welcoming people.

Furthermore, Venezuela boasts an incredible variety of climates and ecosystems, ranging from the humid, tropical rainforests of the Amazon basin to the pristine, sun-drenched beaches of the Caribbean coast. The country also features alpine conditions in the snow-capped peaks of the Andes Mountains, as well as vast, semi-arid plains and desert landscapes in the northwestern region, making it a remarkably diverse territory.
